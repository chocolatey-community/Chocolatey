TOPIC
    about_Chocolatey

SHORT DESCRIPTION
    PowerShell wrapper of choco.exe and DSC resources for deployment and configuration of Chocolatey.

LONG DESCRIPTION
    This module contains DSC resources for deployment and configuration of Chocolatey

EXAMPLES
    PS C:\> Get-Command -Module Chocolatey
    PS C:\> Get-DscResource -Module Chocolatey

NOTE:
    Thank you to all those who contributed to this module, by writing code, sharing opinions, and provided feedback.
